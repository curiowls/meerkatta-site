Features

Everything between your idea and the finished result.

You handle the content. MeerKatta handles everything else.

Voice

Voice-to-Text

Speak naturally into any text field — on Mac or iPhone. MeerKatta transcribes, cleans filler words, and drops polished text at your cursor. Instantly.

App-Aware Tone

MeerKatta detects which app you're in. Formal for email. Casual for Slack. Structured for Notion. The right register, automatically.

Multilingual

Speak in any language. Whisper detects it automatically and transcribes on-device. No language picker. No settings. Just talk.

Video

Smart Trim

AI detects dead air, false starts, and trailing silence. Cuts to the good stuff — automatically.

Auto Captions

Local transcription in English, Chinese, and Japanese. Burned-in subtitles styled for each platform.

Platform-Ready Posts

One recording, a ready-to-publish post for each platform. XHS captions, LinkedIn write-ups, TikTok hooks — tuned to the platform and to your voice. Copy, paste, publish.

Audio Fix

Broadcast-standard normalization. Sound professional without a studio.

Learns You

Remembers your platforms, your caption style, your preferences. Every recording gets smarter.

No Upload. No Wait.

Everything runs on your Mac. No cloud. No queue. Works offline. Processing starts the instant you stop recording.

More

Vocabulary Learner

Auto-learns custom words, names, and jargon from your recordings. Better accuracy over time, without manual dictionaries.

The Bigger Picture

AI is powerful. But power isn't the problem.

The gap isn't capability — it's access. AI can do almost anything, if you ask right. That "if" is the product opportunity of a generation.

The Problem with General AI

General-purpose AI gives you infinite power and zero direction. Every session starts from scratch. You re-explain your style, your platforms, your preferences. The AI can edit your video brilliantly — if you write a perfect prompt. Every. Single. Time.

Specialist Faces

MeerKatta is a specialist. It doesn't do everything — it does two things with depth. It knows video. It knows voice. It knows your platforms and your style. The expertise is crystallized in the product so you don't have to carry it in your head.

Absorbing Complexity

Every great product absorbs the gap between what a system can do and what a human knows to ask for. Google absorbed the internet. Canva absorbed Photoshop. MeerKatta absorbs the gap between AI-powered editing and "I just want to post this" — or "I just want to say this clearly."

The Invisible Tool

The best tools disappear. You don't think about the tool — you think about what you're making. Record → Result. The habit forms. The tool fades. Only the output remains. That's what we're building.
