How it works

Three steps. Zero editing.

You bring the idea. MeerKatta brings the intelligence. The tool disappears — only the result remains.

1

Capture

Video: Click the menu bar icon or press ⌘⇧R. Pick screen, camera, or both.
Voice: Tap into any text field and speak naturally.

2

AI Processes

Video: Trims silence, normalizes audio, burns captions, resizes per platform.
Voice: Removes filler words, adapts tone to the active app, polishes prose.

3

Done

Video: Context-ready files with titles and captions. Preview, share.
Voice: Polished text appears at your cursor. Email, chat, notes — wherever you are.

Features

Everything between your idea and "done"

You handle the content. MeerKatta handles everything else.

Smart Trim

AI detects dead air, false starts, and trailing silence. Cuts to the good stuff — automatically.

Auto Captions

Local transcription in English, Chinese, and Japanese. Burned-in subtitles styled for each platform.

Platform Presets

One recording → multiple outputs. XHS (3:4), LinkedIn (16:9), Instagram (9:16), TikTok, Twitter. All at once.

Audio Fix

Broadcast-standard normalization and noise reduction. Sound professional without a studio.

Learns You

Remembers your platforms, your caption style, your preferences. Every recording gets smarter.

No Upload. No Wait.

Everything runs on your Mac — no uploading to a server, no waiting in a queue. Works offline. Processing starts the instant you stop recording.

Voice to Text

Speak naturally into any text field. MeerKatta transcribes, removes filler words, and polishes your speech into clean prose — inserted at your cursor.

App-Aware Tone

MeerKatta reads which app you're in. Formal for email. Casual for Slack. Structured for Notion. Same voice, right register — automatically.

Multi-Language

Speak Chinese, get English. Or Japanese to Chinese. Local transcription in EN, ZH, and JP with AI-powered translation between them.
