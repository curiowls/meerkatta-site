Decision helper

Two good reasons to buy, two different setups.

Choose Lifetime if...

  • You already pay for an inference service.
  • Your company gives you API keys for work tools.
  • You prefer one-time purchases under $50.
  • You want one less recurring software subscription.

Choose Subscription if...

  • You want MeerKatta to work without AI setup.
  • You do not want to manage API keys or providers.
  • You care more about the capture habit than the AI stack.
  • You are comfortable with a small recurring plan.
